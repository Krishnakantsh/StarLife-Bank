General guideline for Bank CSP (Customer Service Point)

1. Eligibility

Must have completed necessary training as prescribed by the bank.

Should have a valid Aadhaar card, PAN card, and other required KYC documents.

hould possess basic financial and digital literacy.

2. Infrastructure Requirements

A secure and designated service location.

Computer/laptop with internet connectivity.

Biometric device for authentication (as required by the bank).

Printer and scanner for document processing.

CCTV surveillance (if mandated by the bank).

3. Key Responsibilities

Facilitate account opening, deposits, and withdrawals.

Process money transfers and government scheme benefits.

Offer loan application support (if authorized).

Ensure proper customer verification (KYC compliance).

Maintain transaction records and issue receipts.
